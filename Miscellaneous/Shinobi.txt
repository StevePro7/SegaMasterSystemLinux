The computer programmer for the original Shinobi video game, released by Sega in 1987, was Yutaka Sugano, also known by his pseudonym Michael S. Schwartz. Sugano served as the lead developer and designer for the game, contributing significantly to its gameplay mechanics and overall design.

Shinobi became a classic arcade hit, known for its tight controls, iconic ninja protagonist (Joe Musashi), and innovative gameplay elements.
